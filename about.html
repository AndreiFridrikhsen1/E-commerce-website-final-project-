Who we are

Founded in 1986, we have been in the tech industry for almost 35 years. In that time, we have grown from our start as a mail-order operation run out of a two-bedroom apartment to being a chain with five retail stores serving consumers and corporations all around the Bay Area.
Over the decades, we have been part of the evolution of computing and technology and have kept growing while competitors shut down. We feel privileged to develop deep relationships in serving our customers: from gamers to artists to engineers, and small businesses to enterprises like Intel, Nvidia, Samsung, and the City and County of San Francisco.
